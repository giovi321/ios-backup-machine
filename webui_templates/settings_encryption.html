How backup encryption works

  • Encryption is set on the iPhone, not on this device.
  • Once enabled, the iPhone remembers the setting — all future backups are encrypted automatically.
  • The password is only needed to restore a backup, not to create one.
  • The password is never stored on the iOS Backup Machine — it is sent to the iPhone and immediately discarded.
  • If you forget the password, you cannot restore encrypted backups.
  • If the first backup is interrupted after encryption is enabled, the encryption remains active — the next backup will proceed normally.
